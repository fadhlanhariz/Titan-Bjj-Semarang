Tentang Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ) adalah seni bela diri yang bukan hanya tentang kekuatan fisik, tetapi juga tentang kecerdasan dan strategi. BJJ mengajarkan Anda untuk menggunakan teknik dan taktik yang tepat untuk mengalahkan lawan yang lebih besar dan kuat.

BJJ bukan hanya tentang bertarung di atas matras, tetapi juga tentang membangun karakter dan mentalitas juara. BJJ akan membantu Anda menaklukkan ketakutan Anda, meningkatkan kepercayaan diri Anda, dan mencapai potensi maksimal Anda.

Sejarah Terbentuknya Titan BJJ Semarang

Titan BJJ Semarang berawal dari kumpulan praktisi pecinta beladiri untuk berbaagi ilmu, kemudian berkembang dan bergabung dengan Universal Jiu-Jitsu (sekarang SMASH Gym) dibawah Prof Martin Hartono, bergabung dengan Checkmat BJJ dibawah bimbingan Prof. Marco 'Sharpei' Machado hingga akhirnya menjadi Titan BJJ Semarang di bawah pengawasan dan bimbingan Prof Alison Braga dan Prof Bruno Amorim.

Misi Kami

Misi kami adalah untuk menyediakan lingkungan yang aman dan mendukung di mana orang-orang dari segala usia dan kemampuan dapat belajar Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu. Kami percaya bahwa BJJ adalah seni bela diri yang berharga yang dapat membantu orang mengembangkan kebugaran fisik, disiplin mental, dan kepercayaan diri.

Instruktur Kami

Tim instruktur kami sangat berpengalaman dan bersemangat untuk mengajar BJJ. Mereka berkomitmen untuk membantu siswa mencapai tujuan mereka, apakah mereka baru saja memulai atau merupakan pesaing berpengalaman.

Program Kami

Kami menawarkan berbagai program untuk segala usia dan tingkatan, termasuk:

  • Kelas Dewasa: Kelas untuk orang dewasa dari semua tingkatan, dari pemula hingga mahir.
  • Kelas Anak-anak: Kelas untuk anak-anak usia 4-12 tahun.
  • Kelas Wanita: Kelas yang dirancang khusus untuk wanita.
  • Pelajaran Privat: Instruksi yang dipersonalisasi untuk individu atau kelompok kecil.

Fasilitas Kami

Fasilitas kami dilengkapi dengan segala yang Anda butuhkan untuk berlatih BJJ. Kami memiliki area matras yang luas, Kamar mandi yang bersih, dan ruang ganti.

Instruktur Kami

Bruno Amorim

Originally from Belo Horizonte, Brazil, Professor Bruno has been practising the art of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu for over 25 years and has been teaching for 19. A student of the legendary World-Champion maker, Vinicius “Draculino” Magalhaes, Professor Bruno earned his Black Belt on the podium after winning the IBJJF Worlds in 2009. As a competitor, Professor Bruno has a long list of accolades including first place wins at the IBJJF Pan Pacs in Australia in 2019 (three Gold Medal Wins), Copa de Bangkok 2018, Abu Dhabi Grand Slam in Tokyo in 2108 and 2019, four Gold Medal wins at the IBJJF Manila Open (Gi & No-Gi) in 2018, and third place at the prestigious Abu Dhabi World Pro 2019. His most recent wins include 2nd place at the World Pro 2022, 1st place for no-gi in the AJP tour Malaysia 2023 and 2nd place for gi in the AJP tour Singapore 2024. As an instructor, Professor Bruno has a wealth of experience in teaching – from serving as the head coach of the Indonesian National Team for the 2018 Asian Games, training the US military in hand-to-hand combat, to being the children’s program director in several BJJ schools in Singapore. Besides BJJ, Professor Bruno is also a black belt in Judo and an experienced MMA fighter who has competed in promotions in China, Kazakhstan, and Brazil.

Alison Braga

Head Instructor of Gracie Barra Indonesia Professor Alisson “Marmota” Braga is a third degree black belt from Gracie Barra Belo Horizonte under the legendary Vinicius “Draculino” Magalhaes. He has been training BJJ for over 15 years, and received his black belt in 2010. An active competitor in the Martial Art, Professor Alisson has won many national and international titles during his years of training, including 2 CBJJE World Championships and Gold at the IBJJF Manila Open 2018 and 2019. Before coming to Indonesia, Professor Alisson taught BJJ in Brazil and Singapore, and is also a professional physiotherapist, gaining his degree from Pontifical Catholic University of Minas Gerais.F

Hendra 'Sean' Wijaya

Head Instructor of Titan BJJ Semarang Coach Sean, a Purple Belt promoted by Professor Bruno Amorim and Professor Alisson, serves as the Head Coach of Titan BJJ Semarang. He started practicing BJJ in Shanghai BJJ when he was in college, then trained under Professor Martin Hartono’s team in Universal Grappling where he is known as “The Goblin” in No Gi and later trained under Professor Marco “Sharpei” Machado before joining with his now home in Gracie Barra. With a decorated competitive career, he has secured medals in prestigious tournaments including the IBJJF Manila Open, Kuala Lumpur Open, Indonesian Submission Challenge, and Copa Da Malasia, alongside numerous local competitions. Coach Sean’s achievements attest to his profound understanding of jiujitsu and his unwavering dedication to excellence. His coaching philosophy revolves around instilling values of commitment, knowledge, and sportsmanship in his students, empowering them to pursue greatness in their jiujitsu journey both on and off the mat.
